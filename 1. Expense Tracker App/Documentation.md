1. Project Title & Overview
   Expense Tracker App
   Author: [Patil Jayesh]
   Date: 08-Oct-2025
   Version: v1.0

Executive Summary:
Expense Tracker is a web-based app that lets users easily record, update, delete, and analyze their daily spending—from meals and transport to household and business costs. All data is private, stored in the browser, and the interface is optimized for mobile use.

<!-- -------------------------------------- -->

2. Problem Statement & Goals
   Most people either forget small expenses or find it boring to maintain paper diaries and Excel sheets. Existing apps are full of ads or require login. The goal here is to make basic money management:

Quick and registration-free

Available offline

Friendly even for first-time users

Objectives:

Simple daily entry and instant total

Weekly/monthly recap for habit improvement

Maintain spending privacy; no third party

<!-- ------------------------------------------- -->

3. Requirement Specification
   Functional Requirements
   Add, edit, delete expense entries

Show total spending, category-wise breakdown

Filter by date and category

Export data as CSV for backup

UI: Auto-updating, mobile-responsive, and dark/light themes

Non-Functional Requirements
Fast loading (all local files)

Works on latest Chrome, Firefox, mobile browsers

Data remains in browser (localStorage)

Guidance for backup/restore

User Stories
As a student, I want to track my daily canteen and books expense quickly.

As a freelancer, I want to see my business costs per project per month.

As a homemaker, I want to view my family grocery and utility spending in one place.

6. Detailed Functional Description
   Core Data Structure
   json
   {
   "id": "uuid-1696780630000",
   "name": "Metro Train Ticket",
   "amount": 30,
   "category": "Transport",
   "date": "2025-10-08",
   "note": "Office commute"
   }
   id: Unique, generated by timestamp or random

name: Short entry title

amount: Entry value (number)

category: Food, Transport, Shopping, Other

date: YYYY-MM-DD

note: (optional) details

CRUD Operations
Add: Form with validation (must fill name, amount, category, date)

Edit: Pre-fill form, save updates

Delete: Confirmation pop-up

Filter: Dropdowns or date-picker to select category/date, only relevant results shown

Summary Cards
Display running total

Optional: Pie/bar chart for spending by category (future enhancement)
